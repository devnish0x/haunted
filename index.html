The Story

Blackwood Manor, 1887

Lord Alistair Blackwood vanished on All Hallows' Eve, leaving behind only a locked journal and the faint smell of sulfur. The servants fled. The village sealed the road. Decades of silence followed — until the lights returned to the windows, flickering in patterns that no one could explain.

You are an investigator drawn to the inexplicable. Armed with little more than a lantern and your wits, you must uncover the truth buried within the manor's crumbling walls.
